Shoyu Ramen

Our signature shoyu ramen features a savory soy sauce-based broth made from slow-simmered chicken and pork bones. Topped with tender chashu pork belly, fresh bamboo shoots, crispy green onions, a soft-boiled ajitsuke egg, and homemade narutomaki. Served with fresh ramen noodles in a rich, umami-packed broth.

$12.99

Miso Ramen

A warm and comforting miso ramen with a creamy, complex broth blended from aged miso paste and vegetable stock. Topped with fresh corn, melted butter, sprouting bean sprouts, seasoned ground pork, scallions, and a perfectly cooked soft-boiled egg. The silky broth and tender noodles create an unforgettable comfort meal.

$13.99

Tonkotsu Ramen

Indulge in our silky tonkotsu ramen featuring a rich, creamy pork bone broth simmered for over 18 hours to extract maximum flavor and umami. Garnished with tender chashu pork, black wood ear mushrooms (kikurage), fresh green onions, fragrant garlic oil, and a marinated soft-boiled egg. This classic Fukuoka-style bowl is pure comfort in a bowl.

$14.99

Spicy Ramen

For heat lovers! This fiery spicy ramen features a bold chili-infused broth with layers of heat balanced by umami depth. Loaded with seasoned ground pork, crisp bok choy, fresh ginger slices, chili oil drizzle, and a silky soft-boiled egg. Top it with sesame seeds and extra chili flakes for an extra kick. Not for the faint of heart!
